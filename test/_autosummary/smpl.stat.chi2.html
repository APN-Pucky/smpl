smpl.stat.chi2

smpl.stat.chi2(y, f, sigmas=None)

Chi2 - Goodness of Fit

In general, if Chi-squared/Nd is of order 1.0, then the fit is reasonably good. Coversely, if Chi-squared/Nd >> 1.0, then the fit is a poor one.
